Privacy Policy

AlwaysFreeAAC — Effective date: 30 June 2026

AlwaysFreeAAC (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is a free, open-source Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) app. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it on any platform (web, Android, or iOS).

Summary: AlwaysFreeAAC does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

AlwaysFreeAAC does not collect any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any identifying details.

The only data the app stores is saved locally on your device using your browser’s or operating system’s local storage. This includes:

  • App settings (voice selection, speech rate, pitch, volume, grid size, font size, language, theme)
  • Custom communication boards and tiles you create
  • Your choice of which built-in boards are visible

This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.

2. Speech & Text-to-Speech

AlwaysFreeAAC uses the text-to-speech (TTS) engine built into your device or browser to speak text aloud. On Android and iOS, this uses the operating system’s native speech synthesis API. On the web, it uses the browser’s Web Speech API.

Text entered or selected in the app is processed entirely on-device. No text is transmitted to any external server or third party for speech processing.

Note: If you have installed a third-party TTS engine on your device (such as Acapela or Vocalizer), that engine’s own privacy policy governs how it processes speech requests.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

AlwaysFreeAAC does not include any analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or behavioural tracking software. We do not track how you use the app.

4. No Third-Party Services

The app does not connect to any third-party APIs, social networks, or cloud services. No data is shared with third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

AlwaysFreeAAC is designed to be used by people of all ages, including children. The app does not collect any personal information and stores all data locally on the device. It does not knowingly collect or transmit data from or about children.

If a caregiver or parent is setting up the app on behalf of a child, they can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing their browser or app storage through device settings, or by using the app’s built-in board management to delete custom boards.

6. Data Storage and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time:

  • Web (browser): Clear site data from your browser settings (Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data, then select “Cached data and cookies” or “Site data” for this site).
  • Android: Settings → Apps → AlwaysFreeAAC → Storage → Clear data.
  • iOS: Delete the app from your device. This removes all locally stored data. Reinstall from the App Store or via sideloading when you want to use the app again.

7. Open Source

AlwaysFreeAAC is fully open source. You can inspect the complete source code to verify these privacy claims at any time:

https://github.com/Nakuapp/AlwaysFreeAAC

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the “Effective date” at the top of this page and note the change in the repository. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the GitHub repository:

https://github.com/Nakuapp/AlwaysFreeAAC/issues
